About Us

Sandanezwe Incubation Hub is a collaborative, youth-driven non-profit organisation dedicated to building inclusive economic systems that combat poverty and promote sustainable growth. The organisation focuses on empowering young people in rural communities through skills development, youth development initiatives, SMME support, and broader community development programmes. Recognising the socioeconomic exclusion and geographical disadvantages faced by many rural communities, Sandanezwe Incubation Hub provides opportunities, resources, and support that enable young people to develop their potential, participate in economic activities, and build sustainable livelihoods. Through these initiatives, the organisation works to uplift communities and contribute to long-term economic independence and the eradication of poverty in rural areas.
