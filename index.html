About Us

Sandanezwe Incubation Hub is a collaborative, youth-driven non-profit organisation dedicated to building inclusive economic systems that combat poverty and promote sustainable growth. The organisation focuses on empowering young people in rural communities through skills development, youth development initiatives, SMME support, and broader community development programmes.

Our Services

Skills Development

Bridging the skills gap and promoting economic growth by equipping individuals with new competencies that improve employability. We provide targeted training programmes, mentorship, workshops, and learnerships focusing on technical skills, interpersonal abilities, and cognitive development.

Youth Development

Empowering young people through skills development, entrepreneurship support, and job placement opportunities. We collaborate with established development agencies to provide training, create employment pathways, and support youth-owned enterprises.

SMME Support

Assisting youth in establishing and growing sustainable businesses through mentorship, strategic partnerships, and business development support. We work with government and private sector initiatives to provide access to funding opportunities and enterprise growth programmes.

Community Development

Strengthening communities through collaboration, improved access to opportunities, and initiatives that address social inequalities. We identify community needs and mobilise support through corporate social investment and sustainable development partnerships.
